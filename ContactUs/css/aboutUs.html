About Sakr Car Rental

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sakr Car Rental has grown from a small local business to one of the region's most trusted car rental services. What began with just 5 vehicles has now expanded to a fleet of over 200 cars, serving thousands of satisfied customers annually.

We pride ourselves on offering more than just vehicles - we provide complete mobility solutions tailored to your needs. Whether you need a compact car for city driving, a luxury vehicle for special occasions, or an SUV for family trips, we've got you covered.

Our mission is to make car rental simple, affordable, and stress-free. We achieve this through transparent pricing, well-maintained vehicles, and exceptional customer service available 24/7.
